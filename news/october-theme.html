TokyoTreat's October Theme!

05 September 2016 by Vanessa

Once again, it's that time of the month when we reveal October box's theme!

Halloween is a lot of people's favorite holiday and Autumn is a season when many seasonal flavors take over the supermarket aisles. We've decided to put the best of both worlds in your October box so you no longer have to settle for one or the other! We also have a super fun contest that only our subscribers are eligible for! And not to be forgotten, all subscribers are automatically entered for a chance to win Lucky Treat which boasts a wide array of treats and goodies!

Halloween 2016 is sure to be a very fun and satisfying holiday with your monthly fix of tasty Japanese snacks!
Be sure to check out our October box and don't forget, sign up ends on Sept 30th, 2016!

 Any guesses to what kind of seasonal flavors are in your October box? Can't wait to get your hands on our Autumn Flavors and Halloween box? Let us know how excited you are in the comment section!
