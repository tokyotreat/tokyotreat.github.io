Pet lovers, this is the purr-fect contest for you!

04 March 2016 by Vanessa

Say hello to our cutest.contest.EVERRR!!!! It is time for you to show off your beloved pets (cats, dogs, birds, reptiles, animal plushies, we don't judge!). Enter the cutest, goofiest, most awkward photo of your pet with their TokyoTreat boxes. It can be a picture of them drooling all over the candies or hogging the treats-- you and your pet's imagination is the only limit.

Follow these simple steps to enter:
1. Enter your entries during March 1st – March 26th
2. Take the most creative photo of your pet while striking a pose with a TokyoTreat box. Upload it on Instagram/Twitter with hashtag #tokyotreat AND #petscravetreats. Don't have a pet? Enter your favorite animal plush toy!!
3. Upload as many photos as you like to increase your chances of winning. Please note, if you keep your profile private, then we won’t be able to see your entry.

Prizes:

1st Prize : 3 month Premium subscription and Doraemon’s special toy set.

2nd Prize : 2 month Premium subscription

3rd Prize : 1 month Premium subscription.

Now prepare yourself for some cuteness overload, as we share you some pictures of our team member's pet!

Seriously, how can you say no to that face?

SOO ADORABLE, right?!!

We can't wait to see your entries!!! Start tagging them now!
