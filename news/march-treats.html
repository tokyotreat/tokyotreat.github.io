Fruity Flavors in March's TokyoTreat!

02 April 2016 by Vanessa

This month's box is all about fruity flavors! We greeted our subscribers with some of our all time favorites such as Melon Cream Soda, Honey Hi-Chew, Peach Caramel Corn Hinamasturi Edition, BBQ Scones and more! Continue reading below to see the full details on each snacks!

BONUS SNACK!

Who doesn't love bonuses? Especially if it's something sweet and delicious! Every March Premium box came with a bonus snack! Out of these all time favorites, which one did you get?

Morinaga BAKE Cookies & Cream Ice Cream

If you love Japanese snacks, then you should be familiar with BAKE from Morinaga. These cookie like snacks are incredibly popular in Japan due to the delicious texture. It's crispy on the outside and stuffed with soft creamy filling on the inside.

Hello Kitty Hinamatsuri Hana Arare

For those of you who don't know, Hinamatsuri is "Girl's Day" in Japan. To celebrate this day, families often eat snacks such as Hana Arare. This month we're bringing you a special Hello Kitty edition Hana Arare. These sweet rice puffs are so much fun to eat!

Cream Collon Vanilla

Collon is another popular biscuit snack in Japan! You'll understand why once you've tried it.  These heavenly creamy biscuits are suited to all occasions. How can you go wrong with crunchy wafer biscuit with a smooth vanilla flavoured cream filling? You simply can't.

SPECIAL ITEM!!

Reality Sushi Magnet

These sushi magnets look so real, you could actually fool someone with it! (We tried and managed to trick one of our staff!) Try not to stare at it for too long, it might make you go hungry. Which sushi magnet did you get? Let us know in the comments below!

Melon Cream Soda

This creamy drink is definitely one of the favorites among our subscribers. We were so happy to hear that people were loving it as much as we did! Once you take a sip of this cream soda, putting it down will be a challenge. We don't blame you, this drink tastes like melon and a scoop of ice cream mixed together -- which is perfect for a hot day!

Kracie's Kneading Land DIY Candy Kit

This DIY Candy Kit is one of our favorite candy kits from Kracie. Put on your creative hats and mold these grape flavored candies into a masterpiece -- or you know maybe some cute animals, cars, people, or whatever you prefer.

Peach Caramel Corn (Hinamatsuri Edition)

Here's another limited edition snack to celebrate Hinamatsuri! We all know how delicious Caramel Corns are, but we never knew that they could be THAT good in peach flavor. Open the package, and you'll immediately be greeted with a sweet fruity aroma.

BBQ Flavored Scones

Of course we had to include a tasty savory snack to balance out all the sweets. Imagine a delicious smokey BBQ bread stick with the  perfect level of crunchiness. We can't help but drool just at the thoughts of it. They are sooo good!

Trust us.

Alfort Blonde Chocolate -- Milk Flavor

For those of you who don't know, Alfort is a premium chocolate brand by Bourbon. The combination of biscuit and creamy milk makes it a tasty experience! These are surprisingly satisfying and perfect for when you are on the go!

Honey Kumquat Hi-Chew

Everyone knows Hi-Chew, but not everyone can get their hands on the honey kumquat flavor. Honey Kumquat is a limited edition flavor, and like all good things -- these aren't available for long. We hope you enjoy the chewiness, deliciousness and juiciness of this candy!

Tyrol Sakura Shiriejo

March in Japan is the season of sakura (cherry blossoms) and that means snack manufacturers are releasing a line of limited edition sakura treats. We wouldn't be able to live with ourselves if we didn't include a delicious sakura treat in your boxes.  So please enjoy this candy and enjoy a whiff of spring!

Bourbon Usuyaki Mix

Another delicious savory snack that we think you would love! Thin crispy rice crackers with 3 different flavors: onion, tomatoes, and spinach. Crispy, tasty, and make you happy! Which flavor did you like best?

Punifua Grape Paw Gummies

Dog and cat paw shaped gummies packed with a juicy grape flavor. Not only are they deliciously sweet, but so much fun to eat due to it's adorable packaging and shape! We've got to hand it to the Japanese for not only paying attention to the taste, but also the experience people get when eating them!

Hello Kitty Strawberry Sticks

For all Sanrio lovers, we know you'll love these special Hello Kitty pocky-like snack! This is the perfect treat to share with anyone, anytime, anywhere. Although, it might be a good idea to keep them to yourselves.... they're really good.

Strawberry Cotton Candy

A twist on a classic, delicious strawberries are perfectly balanced with the sweet flavor of cotton candy. What more can you ask for?

Strawberry Taiyaki

These Taiyaki are filled with a soft bubbly Strawberry filling, covered by a light wafer shell. We know you'll enjoy the crunch of the outside and the creaminess on the inside. It's definitely addictive!

Strawberry Wheat Chocolate

Takaoka never fails to impress. This sweet crunchy snack is wheat biscuit dipped in thick cream strawberry coating. You might have to restrain yourself from eating them in one sitting. Yes, that's how good they are.

-------

It might be hard to choose, but what are your favorites from March's box? Let us know!

We hope that you have enjoyed everything so far and are looking forward to future boxes!
