Bizarre Candies Edition: June Goodies Revealed

05 July 2016 by Vanessa

Venture into the world of bizarre Japanese candies with TokyoTreat's June box! 

Chimokke Mascot

Cute and quirky Chimokke mascots that are ready to accompany you anywhere! Chimokke is a popular character in Japan that is said to come from the Mokekenoke star. These irresistibly cute alien characters come with a handy strap, so you can attach it to your bag or pouch.

DIY Mysterious Coris Syrup

This popular DIY candy kit contains 3 kinds of syrup. The red one has a strawberry flavor, the blue one has a soda flavor, and the yellow flavor is lemon flavored. If you mix them together, they mysteriously change their flavor to grape! Here's your chance to try something new and fun with only a few simple steps!

Sakura Cola

Need a refreshing drink to enjoy during the summer time? We’ve got just the thing for you! Because the Sakura Pepsi from our April box was such a HUGE hit, we decided to bring you the Cola version! Enjoy it while cold and you’ll instantly feel refreshed by the sweet floral aroma! Do you prefer Sakura Cola or Pepsi Sakura?

Limited Edition Chocolate Umaibo

Umaibos are puffed cylindrical corn snacks that are extremely popular in Japan. It’s tasty, crunchy, and simple to snack on! Different from the usual Umaibos which are savory, this limited edition Umaibo is coated in sweet chocolate. It’s time to indulge the chocoholic in you.

KitKat Otona Crush and Spread

This month we’re bringing you a more elegant version of KitKat! The Otona Crush and Spread KitKat features dark chocolate cookie crumbles layered between the wafers. The bittersweet qualities of this elegant KitKat suitably accent the chocolate’s creamy base and creates the perfect balance of sweet and bitter.

Koikeya's Potato Tiramisu Chips

For those craving the crispy AND the sweet, we introduce Koikeya’s Tiramisu Chips! What’s unique about these chips is that they can change flavors from savory to sweet! The chips start off as cheese flavored, but with a simple sprinkle of powder, they turn into a tasty tiramisu flavor! How exciting!!

Fujiya Chocolate Pencils

Fujiya not only focuses on the quality of their chocolate, but as you can see, their packaging is always unique! These chocolate treats are shaped like pencils and taste incredibly creamy and delicious! To add to the fun, they also come with a random alphabet sticker!

Chocolate Dipped Corn

Satisfy your sweet tooth with this pack of cute star shaped corn puffs, dipped in a coating of rich chocolate. Crunchy, light and wholesome, chocolaty and delicious, this corn puff snack is a perfect anytime treat! Warning: it’s highly addictive!

Tohato's Neko Atsume Biscuit

Tohato collaborates with the popular Neko Atsume to bring you a deliciously sweet biscuit snack! Shaped like the cute cats of Neko Atsume, they are perfect to munch on at any time of the day. Please enjoy the goodness of tasty milk caramel flavor in every bite!

Crazy Bubbly Soda Ramune Candy

Due to popular demand, the Ramune Candies are back! But now, it’s served with a twist!! This outrageously fun and yummy candy will instantly generate lots of foam once you pop them into the mouth! Surprise your friends with this candy and see how they react in shock!

Yaokin Big Ika

This mildly spicy squid snack is a very popular dagashi liked by young and old in Japan. Satisfy your need for savory-ness, with this traditional snack that is packed with flavor! We think this is perfect to enjoy while you’re on the go!

Hi-Eight Chocolate

Hi-Eight Chocolate by Furuta is a classic sweet enjoyed by many Japanese families since 1967. It has a great harmony of smooth creamy milk chocolate covered with a sweet, colorful candy coating. Children usually attach threads through holes on the sides to make fun glasses!

Smartphone Mochi

As most of you might already know, Mochi is one of the most popular tradition sweet in Japan! They are small, tasty and easy to just pop in your mouth whenever you need a sweet snack! We hope you enjoy sharing this delicious sweet and chewy mochi treat with your friends and family!

Pine’s Cola Lollipop

Are you a fan of unique flavored lollipops? Then you’re going to have a lot of fun! This lollipop is cola flavored while the powder is a sweet delicious sherbet. Dip your lollipop into the tasty powder and enjoy the fizzling sensation in your mouth!

Karaoke Mic Ramune

Ramune candies have been around for many, many years, and due to its overwhelming popularity, they are made into many different forms and packaging. Now, you can sing your favorite Japanese songs with this Karaoke microphone and enjoy the yummy ramune candies inside.

Super Long HimoQ Gummy

You are now in the possession of the longest gummy in candy history!! This Cola and Soda flavored candy is an outstanding 126cm long!! Sweet and with a slightly carbonated taste, this is definitely a fun candy to share with a friend. Now, the question is: how long will it take for you to finish it?

Don’t miss out on all the delicious Japanese goodies and subscribe now!
