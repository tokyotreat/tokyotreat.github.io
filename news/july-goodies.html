TokyoTreat July Goodies Revealed!

04 August 2016 by Vanessa

For the month of July, we wanted to bring you those nostalgic memories and take you back to the good old days when you watched your favorite Anime on TV. Nothing beats anime watching with snacks featuring your favorite characters! Here are the complete list of snacks included in July's box!

Brazilian Orange Pocky (Summer Limited edition)

We’re taking you on a mini trip to Brazil with Pocky’s newest exclusive edition for this summer season! With a special Brazilian flavor of tropical orange, this perfectly sweet and citrus coating will liven up any mouth. We hope you love this light and tasty treat!

Milk-flavored Potato Chips

We bet you’ve never tasted anything like this! We’re excited to bring you the newly released Milk-flavored Chips by Koikeya. Not only do they smell and taste like dairy, they also have just as much calcium as one would expect in a glass of milk, making it a nutritious morning snack. Who knew you could have potato chips for breakfast?!

Shrimp Crackers

We think Japanese Shrimp Crackers make a fantastic alternative to your ordinary crisps. A classic snack with excellent savory taste and crunch, these yummy Shrimp Crackers are perfect to accompany you while you watch your favorite shows.

Calpis Gummy

Once you open this pack, you’re instantly hit with a smell of sweet milk. These soft gummy candies are flavored after the popular Calpis drink, with a pocket of yogurt flavored syrup inside to give them even more flavor. It also comes in a re-sealable foil packet to keep things fresh.

Moko Moko Mokolet Toilet DIY Candy 3

With this hilarious DIY candy kit you can assemble your own toilet and decorate it with super fun stickers! The candy comes in a sweet soda and cola flavor that can turn into an interesting foamy sweet drink bubbling out of the toilet bowl!!

Marutake Peach Drink

Need a refreshing drink to enjoy during the summer time? We’ve got just the thing for you! Try this Marutake Peach Drink and you’ll instantly feel refreshed by the sweet fruity aroma! For extra enjoyment, you can throw them in the freezer for a future, frosty treat.

Thomas & Friends Chocolate Pretzel Sticks

If you're looking for a satisfying way to indulge, the combination of crunchy pretzel sticks drenched in smooth, creamy chocolate won't let you down. Featuring Thomas & Friends, it’s a fun snack for an afternoon treat!

 Anpanman Ramune Candy

Do you love Anpanman as much as we do? Check out these tasty ramune candies featuring Anpanman and his friends! They come in a variety of fruity flavors including grape, peach, apple and orange. Crunch right into them or let it dissolve in your mouth.

Yokai Watch Chocolate

This comforting treat allows you to enjoy a subtle and tasty chocolate lollipop shaped like your favorite "Yokai Watch" character. Satisfy your sweet tooth and let this yummy chocolate slowly melt in your mouth. 

Pokemon Gummy

You’re going to have a lot of fun with this unique, taste-changing gummy! Packed in a cute Pokemon packaging, comes 3 delicious flavors; pineapple, grape and peach. Enjoy the flavors separately or be adventurous and combine them and see what fruity flavors you come up with!

Thomas and Friends Chewing Candy

Another Thomas and Friends treat from us to you! But this time it’s a sweet, fruity chewing gum! Delicious grape flavor bursts from these juicy candies. Each pack comes with a collectible Thomas and Friends wrapper.

Dragon Ball Heroes Snack 5

This is a rich and crispy snack that has just the right amount of flavor to make it irresistible! It has a delicious creamy and buttery cheese taste that will make it hard for you to put down! Not only that, every pack comes with an awesome Dragon Ball Heroes collectable card.

Cocotama Snack

Are you familiar with Himitsu no Cocotama? It’s currently a very popular animation program in Japan! Every lovable character needs to have a snack product of their own! Give these cute crispy milk and strawberry biscuits a try and see how you like them!

Soybean Flour Mochi Rice Cake DIY

A famous New Year's tradition in Japan is eating warm, freshly pounded mochi under a thick dusting of roasted soybean flour. We’re bringing you something similar in the form of a simple DIY kit!

Fortune-telling Chocolate Pills (Dagashi Kashi)

Feeling a little under-the-weather? Take a dose of these delicious colorful chocolate pills! Made from creamy milk chocolate, this yummy treat will make you feel a whole lot better! Don’t forget to check your fortune once you pop out the candies!

Black Thunder Gold

Black Thunder by Yuraku is one of the most popular chocolate bars in Japan. Once you give it a try, you’ll easily see why! It’s an exciting combination of Japanese-style rice puffs and dark crispy cookie center, surrounded by rich milk chocolate. This Gold version combines the strong flavor of coconut and it works beautifully!

What was your favorite this month?
