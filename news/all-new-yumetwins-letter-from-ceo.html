All NEW YUMETWINS - Letter From CEO

01 December 2016 by Ayumi

Hi everyone!

This is Ayumi, the founder and CEO of TokyoTreat Group. I want to thank you foremost, for always using our TokyoTreat, YumeTwins and TokyoTreat Shop services! Because of all your continuous support, we’ve been able to come this far and expand in so many different ways!

We are happy to announce that on 12/2/2016 4PM JST, we will complete our renewal of the YumeTwins brand. We couldn’t have done it with you guys!

We not only redesigned our website but we’ve completely revamped our box and improved the quality of the content of our box!

Since the birth of YumeTwins in May 2016, we’ve been showered with unconditional love and even though we’re still only 6 months young, we’ve been able to grow at an incredible rate as a brand.

As a brand, we’re always trying to better ourselves by listening to our most important critics, our subscribers. We conducted a large-scale survey to hear what our subscribers like and want to see in their future boxes and how we can keep improving our service to satisfy all our subscribers.

Our philosophy has always been and will continue to be “to introduce the world to high quality Japanese products.”

That being said, the biggest request in our survey was to have less items but with better quality and practical items. And we too learned that we can significantly increase the total value of your box that way! This allows us to bring you exclusive merchandise and premium items while maintaining the same subscription price!

With your requests in mind, here’s what you can expect to find in our new and improved YumeTwins box!

 - 5 Kawaii items Including:

  •  Premium plushie
  •  Lifestyle goods (*New!* Check out our newly reopened website at yumetwins.com launching in 24 hours)
  •  Premium anime character merchandise guaranteed

 - FREE worldwide shipping

 - Value over $50!

We want to bring our subscribers higher quality items than ever before!

For example, every box will feature at least 1 premium quality plushie that is larger and of higher quality than ever before! To hold the larger plushie and other bigger items, we've completely redesigned and enlarged our box that it is now nearly 2x times bigger than the previous box.

Popular Japanese character will make an appearance in every box.

We asked, and you told us! Gathering the data from the survey we held, below are the most popular characters with our subscribers and you can count at least one of them making an appearance in your box each month!

  •  Sailor Moon
  •  Rilakkuma
  •  Hello Kitty
  •  Pokemon
  •  Studio Ghibli
  •  Doraemon
  •  Gudetama

We promise you that we’ll only ever feature authentic, fully licensed merchandises in our box!

Just imagine, your home filled with all sorts of kawaii Japanese characters!

Better, faster and more robust delivery!

YumeTwins uses the service of Japan Post to deliver your box every month. Japan Post is Japan’s oldest and largest postal service and maintains strong partnerships with other postal services around world.

Just in time for the new YumeTwins, we’ve gone ahead and strengthened our partnership with Japan Post to ensure that all of our subscribers receive their boxes safely and in a more timely manner than in the past.

Best of all, the price remains the same!

In order to bring you the new YumeTwins, that will be loved by even more people, we’ve gone through many major changes. Everything from the website, the box, box content, delivery but one thing remains the same - the price!

All of us at YumeTwins hope from the bottom of our hearts, that you enjoy the new and improved YumeTwins and will continue to support this kawaii-filled journey with us!

Ayumi
