Ready, Set, WEFIE!!

06 April 2016 by Vanessa

Get together with friends or family and take a wefie (a.k.a group selfie) with your TokyoTreat boxes and grab the chance to win awesome prizes!!

Here’s how you can enter:

1. Tag #WefieTT and #TokyoTreat on Twitter/Instagram/Facebook. If your account is private, we won't be able to see your entry.

2. Take the photo with 2 or more people, and must include TokyoTreat box.

3. Upload as many entries as you’d like to increase your chances of winning!

4. Contest ends on April 29th

Prizes

1st Place: 3 month Premium subscription, Sailor Moon Figure, Chibi Moon Plush

2nd Place: 2 month Premium subscription

3rd Place: 1 month Premium subscription

Here's our wefie, where's yours? ^_^
