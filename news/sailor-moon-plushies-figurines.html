Sailor Moon Plushies Are Adorable, But Check Out These Amazing Figurines!

21 August 2017 by Nic

Here at Yume Twins, we absolutely love Sailor Moon! Sailor Moon plushies, pens, you name it! So, when we stumbled upon these absolutely enchanting figurines, we knew we needed to share them with you! The figurines are 2.8 inches and are part of the Posket Petite Volume 2 series. So, you know what that means! There is a Volume 1 as well! That is wonderful news to us as we love to collect all the kawaii goodness! Okay, okay. I am sure you want to see the figurines now. Without further ado, check out some of the figurines below!

The first figurine is everyone's favorite Sailor Moon! As you can see, she is stunning while also completely adorable. Her eyes make our hearts absolutely melt!

Next, we have Sailor Jupiter! Even though she is all dolled up, her pose gives off that aura of strength she is famous for!

Last, we have Sailor Venus. We love the colors as they help portray Sailor Venus's love for gold and her association to light!

So, did these kawaii figurines charm you too?! We here definitely plan on adding these to our personal collection as soon as possible! By the way, the figurines can be found through Amazon at this link. Tell us who your favorite Sailor Soldier is in the comments below!
