TokyoTreat | October Goodies Revealed

04 November 2016 by Vanessa

October's Halloween x Autumn Japanese Candies

Halloween is a lot of people’s favorite holiday and Autumn is a season when many seasonal flavors take over the supermarket aisles. We’ve decided to put the best of both worlds in your October box so you no longer have to settle for one or the other! Here's a full look at all the Japanese candies included:

Halloween Themed Chopsticks

Whether they are used as eating utensils or as hair accessories, chopsticks are convenient to have around. For the month of October, we decided to add Halloween themed chopsticks into your box. Which color chopsticks did you get?

TokyoTreat Original Halloween Sticker

Since October is Halloween month, we decided to do something very special by creating our first original sticker. What do you think about our original Halloween sticker? Would you like to see more original items in your box in the future?

Tropicana Season’s Best - Mix Fruit Juice

Since we like to put in the most seasonal items in your box, we’ve decided to bring you a special mix fruit juice by Tropicana this month. This juice blends apples, peaches, grapes, oranges and the highly seasonal persimmons to give you an elegant and autumn twist to your mix juice.

Nyoki Nyoki Cororon DIY

We bring you another fun DIY snack this month with Nyoki Nyoki Cororon. These DIY candies come in three different flavors - grape, melon and orange. All you need is a little water and you have yourself colorful worm like candies ready to be eaten in minutes. Which flavor will you try first?

Pipo Chocolate

Small sugar coated chocolates encased in a toy pipe. A toy snack that will surely bring back memories of when you were younger! What’s not to like about that!?

Koala's March Halloween Edition

With all the different cute koala illustrations and the yummy chocolate flavor, it’s easy to understand why Koala’s March has stood the test of time in maintaining its popularity among Japanese snack lovers. This month, we bring you the Halloween edition which features cute Halloween koala illustrations. You can even cut out parts of the package to make a DIY paper glasses!

Kinoko No Yama - Chestnut Chocolate Autumn Edition

Another popular chocolate biscuit snack, these mushroom shaped snacks are a staple on Japanese convenience stores shelves. This month, we bring you the Autumn Edition which combines the regular chocolate with the warm chestnut flavor that can only be found in fall.

Uncle Pierre's Strawberry Cream Roll Cake

This fluffy and sweet strawberry cream roll cake makes for a great light breakfast or a snack or even as a dessert. What time of the day do you plan to enjoy this roll cake?

Hip Hop Berry Soft Candy Stick

Enjoy two flavors with this stick shaped soft candy. Soda flavor on the outside and berry flavor on the inside. Which flavor do you like better? Or are the flavors better when they are mixed?

Wasabi Nori Taro

No Japanese snack box is complete without a touch of traditional mixed in with the new. This month’s traditional flavoring is wasabi. Wasabi Nori Taro is a dried fish snack that packs a punch with the wasabi flavoring. Can your mouth handle the heat?

Bubbly Salty Lemonade Puccho Gummy

Recently, in Japan, it is becoming more common to see fruit snack flavors with a touch of salt to give it an extra dimension. This month we have the bubbly lemonade flavor gummy with a pinch of salt added to give it an extra oomph to your tastebud.

O'Zack Hot Carnival Chili Lime Flavor

O’Zack, the popular chips maker hits a homerun with these zesty and tangy Chili Lime flavor chips that will have everyone feeling nostalgic for those long hot summer days. How do you like these bright flavored chips?

Onion Taro

Corn puff snacks are always popular because they are light but flavorful and got a great texture to them and come in all different flavors. As the name states, this month we bring you delicious onion flavored corn puffs. These yummy onion flavor and light texture are sure to make these snacks super addictive.

Peppered Soybean Jerky

These slightly spicy and salty peppered soybean jerkies are a great solution for when you’re craving something savory. They have the same chewy texture as the real thing, you’ll almost forget that you are eating a soy bean jerky! We think this will definitely be a hit with all of our vegetarian fans!

Premium Hi-Chew - Chardonnay White Grape Flavor

Enjoy the elegant flavor of Chardonnay white grapes with these premium Hi-Chews. The center of these chewy candies is made with Chardonnay grape juice while the outside coating is made with the juice of Muscat of Alexandria to give you a more luxurious flavor and texture compared to the regular Hi-Chews.

Look Purple Sweet Potato and Sweet Chestnut Chocolate

Purple sweet potato and sweet chestnut - two of Autumn’s finest flavors in one box. Enjoy each of the rich flavors coated in chocolate goodness. We think both flavors are sure to be popular but which flavor do you like better?

What was your favorite snack from this month’s box? Let us know in the comments below!
