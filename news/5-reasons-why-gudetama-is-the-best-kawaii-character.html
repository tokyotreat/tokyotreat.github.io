5 reasons why Gudetama is the best kawaii character

31 January 2019 by Paolo

Hello Kitty, Rilakkuma, Monchhichi, Doraemon, Pikachu, Totoro... our favorite will always be Gudetama. And here you can find the 5 reasons why he is the best.

1. He is thicc

Undeniably, Gutedama has the best buttocks of every kawaii characters ever. It is rounded and soft. That's the way we like it.

2. He just needs a rice bowl to live

If you wanna have him as a pet, just give him fresh rice daily, and he will be happy!

3. Lot of proteins

He is made of high quality proteins and vitamins. He's the healthiest Japanese mascot!

4. He never gives up

Ok, basically he is a lazy egg. But everyday he tries hard. We all can relate!

5. Gudetama collaborations are the best

McDonald, Godzilla, Highland Park Bowl, Milky... All his collaborations are cute and awesome!
