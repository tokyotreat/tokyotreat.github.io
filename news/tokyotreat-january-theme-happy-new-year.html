05 December 2016 by Miki

January's Theme - Happy New Year!

Since January is the 1st month of the year, we want everyone to start 2017 right with our Happy New Year box! With all the excitement that the past month brought to TokyoTreat and YumeTwins, you might have thought we'd slack off on our January boxes but worry not! Our January box is filled with lots of goodies that we are sure that both our loyal subscribers and first time subscribers will love! Expect to see red colored items and fizzy snacks that will make you think of New Year. That's not all! We've also included a super fun item that we think you'll get a good laugh out of and which is also used as a prop for the January photo contest! Let's also not forget the Lucky Treat contest that ALL subscribers are automatically entered into! The TokyoTreat January box brings a lot of exciting snacks and fun events all subscribers can look forward to!
