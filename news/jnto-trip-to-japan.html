Win A Trip To Japan - Official Rules

01 December 2017 by Nic

Here are the official rules for the Win a Trip to Japan Campaign!

No Purchase Necessary

1) DESCRIPTION OF THE CAMPAIGN.The "Discover Japan Recipe Campaign" (the "Campaign") is sponsored by Japan National Tourism Organization, 60 East 42nd Street, Suite 448, New York, NY 10165 ("Sponsor"). The winner of the Campaign will receive a Grand Prize Package as more specifically described below. The Contest begins at 12:00 a.m. Eastern Time ("ET") on November 1st, 2017 and ends at 11:59 ET on February 28th, 2018 (the "Campaign Period"). Sponsor will select the Campaign winner in accordance with the procedures set forth below and will announce such winner before March 26th, 2018. By participating in the Campaign, each entrant unconditionally accepts and agrees to comply with and abide by these Official Rules and the decisions of Sponsor, which shall be final and binding in all respects.

2) WHO MAY ENTER.The Campaign is open only to those legal residents of the 50 United States, and the District of Columbia, who are (1) 18 years of age or older at the time of entry and (2) not a member of SAG, AFTRA or any other guild that would prohibit entrant from rendering the services contemplated herein. Directors, officers and employees, and their immediate family members, or those living in the same household as such directors, officers and employees, of Sponsor, Japan National Tourism Organization ("JNTO") or their respective parent, affiliated, or subsidiary companies, agents or representatives (collectively "Promotion Entities") are not eligible to enter or win. The Campaign is void where restricted or prohibited by law.

3) HOW TO ENTER.To participate in the Campaign, you must access the campaign website below.

http://us.jnto.go.jp/food/

During the Campaign Period, you need to enter your email address and vote in the recipe campaign in order to be considered eligible. Entrants will select one of three recipes to vote on. Newsletter signup is optional and not required for eligibility.

4) WINNER SELECTION / JUDGING.At the end of the Campaign, the Grand Prize Winner will be randomly selected among Entrants who voted for the most popular recipe. Winners for 2nd, 3rd and 4th prize will be selected among any of the remaining Entrants.

5) PRIZES.The Grand Prize consists of a round-trip coach airfare(for two) departing from and returning to a major international airport in the United States subject to Sponsor's discretion and is non-transferable and non-exchangeable.

All other expenses of the trip not specified herein shall be the sole responsibility of Prize Winner including, without limitation, accommodation, gratuities, baggage fees, meals and transportation not provided by Sponsor(including, but not limited to, transportation costs to and from the major international airport and Prize Winner's residence), and/or any personal purchases. The travel dates must fall between March 1, 2018 - February 28th, 2019.

Travel must be booked and confirmed by June 30th, 2018 through airlines chosen by the Sponsor's subcontractor, JTB USA Inc.,("Travel Agent"). If the potential prizewinner does not book and provide proof of booking to JTB USA Inc., by June 30th 2018, then such potential prizewinner shall forfeit the prize in its entirety and an alternate prizewinner may be selected from the remaining eligible entries received. The prize winner's travel companion must be 18 years or older.

The prize restrictions/conditions stated herein are not all inclusive and the prize described above may be subject to additional restrictions/conditions, which may be stated in the "Affidavit/Publicity Release" (as defined below) and/or other travel documents.

The Grand Prize Winner and his or her travel companion must travel together on the same itinerary and must possess all required travel documents, including visas and valid passports, if and as applicable. Airfare may not include government taxes, Passenger Facility Charge or September 11th Security Fee. Airline tickets are non-refundable/non-transferable and are not valid for upgrades and/or frequent flyer miles. Other travel restrictions and blackout dates may apply. All airline tickets are subject to the vagaries of flight variation, work stoppages, and schedule or route changes.

Airline ticket arrangements shall be provided by Sponsor's subcontractor, JTB USA Inc., ("Travel Agent") located at 19700 Mariner Avenue, Torrance CA 90503. Travel Agent is responsible for arranging Winner and his or her companion's airline tickets according to these official rules. Grand Prize flight arrangements must be made through the Sponsor's designated Travel Agent contact which will be notified to the Winner by email. The Travel Agent acts solely as a third party travel arranger and Winner and his or her companion agree to release and hold harmless Travel Agent from and against any claim or cause of action, including, but not limited to, personal injury, death, or damage to or loss of property, arising out of participation in this Campaign.

2nd prize is 1 (one) Ito-en Green Tea Gift Pack for 20 (twenty) winners.

3rd prize is 1 (one) Otafuku Sauce Gift Pack for 20 (twenty) winners.

4th prize is 1 (one) Japanese Snack Set: One Month Subscription for 20 (twenty) winners.

2nd, 3rd, and 4th prizes will be sent out to winner's residence by March 26th, 2018 through Sponsor's subcontractor, JTB USA Inc.,

All costs, including federal, state and local taxes, insurance, and other expenses not specifically identified as included in the Grand Prize, are the sole responsibility of the winner. No transfer, assignment, cash redemption or substitution of prizes (or portion of prize) except by Sponsor due to prize unavailability and then for a prize (or applicable portion of prize) of equal or greater value as determined by Sponsor. All details and other restrictions of prizes not specified in these Official Rules will be determined by Sponsor in its sole discretion.

Winners may be required to execute and return an Affidavit of Eligibility, Liability/Publicity Release within seven (7) days following the date of attempted notification. If a selected winner cannot be contacted, is ineligible, fails to claim a prize, and/or fails to return any Affidavit of Eligibility and Liability/Publicity Release (if required), within the applicable time period, Sponsor may elect to disqualify the selected winner, who will then forfeit his or her right to receive the Prize Package.
