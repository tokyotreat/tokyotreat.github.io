April Treats (Pepsi Sakura) - Anniversary Edition!

06 May 2016 by Vanessa

We just had to celebrate our 1st Anniversary with something awesome and the hyped Pepsi Sakura was the perfect thing to kick everything off! Followed by delicious Ramune Candies, Baby Star Udon, Crunchy Panda, Koala March, Calbee BBQ Crisps and many MORE!

Pepsi Sakura

We know how everyone has been hyped about this drink, and we decided to bring the limited edition Pepsi Sakura to April Premium boxes!! The drink itself is a soft, pink color and has a sweet sakura aroma that rolls right off the tongue. It is sure to bring springtime to your taste buds!

 Hello Kitty and Doraemon Purse

The famous characters Doraemon and Hello Kitty has teamed up to create a variety of cute purses to hold your coins or personal items!! Who's team are you on? Hello Kitty? Doraemon? Or, perhaps both??

DIY Green Tea Mochi Kit

Create the popular, traditional Japanese dessert Mochi with this fun and simple DIY kit! Simply add a few drops of water to the mochi discs, dip it in the creamy chocolate and coat it with delicious green tea powder.

Baby Star Bonito Kelp Udon

Satisfy your hunger with this delicious popular noodle meal. This cup noodle comes with a flavour sachet full of classic udon noodle soup seasonings (including bonito fish extract and seaweed extract) and vegetable garnishes that add extra flavour and texture. Yummy don noodles will be yours to enjoy in mere minutes!!

Meiji's Pucca Chocolate

Pucca Choco Biscuits by Meiji are cute sea creature shaped biscuits filled with smooth chocolate cream filling.These tasty Pucca Biscuits come in a cool packaging and are a great treat to share with friends!

Calbee BBQ Flavored Sapporo Potato Snacks

Calbee are Japan’s leading makers of potato snacks, and we are proud to bring you their latest release. This large bag is packed with big fluffy pieces of BBQ flavor chips.Made with Potatoes from the Sapporo region of Japan, and coated with an authentic BBQ flavor.

Raw Ramune Candies

Three words to perfectly describe this delicious Ramune Candy; sweet, fizzy, refreshing!! These cube shaped candies taste amazingly like the actual Ramune itself! You can even try freezing them in the fridge for extra enjoyment!

Chocolate Monaca

Delicious biscuit snack filled with creamy chocolate and crispy rice crisps! This Chocolate Monaca has a texture that is wonderfully crisp and crunch. Perfect to enjoy with a hot cup of tea!

Scissor-Cut Grilled Seaweed

What makes the taste of this grilled seaweed so amazingly good? That's for you to find out! This salty, crispy, delicious snack has been a favorite for as long as we remember!

Carrot Chopped Rice Cake

Mini crunchy rice crisps in a adorable carrot packaging? Yes, please! This unique snack is quite light, sweet and baked to perfection! So much fun to enjoy with friends and family!

Meiji's Plum Pati-Chan

If you are a candy enthusiast, then you'll know what this is far beyond your ordinary candy. Not only is the flavor unique but the texture makes it so much fun to eat! You have to admit these tiny candy plum flavored crystals pack a neat little punch. This candy takes us straight back to our childhood!

Crunchy Panda Z

Rich creamy chocolate biscuits in the shape of adorable Pandas! This extremely delicious snack is made by famous Japanese snack manufacturer Kabaya, and look as sweet as they taste. We dare you not to eat the whole box!

Karamucho Hot Chili-Flavored Chips

If you are tempted by chilli hot and very hot crisps then we have the hot potato chips for you. This Karamucho Hot Chilli Chips has a tongue-tingling, nose-tickling kick of flavor!

Baby Star Yakisoba Sauce Flavor

We introduce to you everyone's all time favorite noodle snack! The combination of bit sized ramen noodles and savory Yakisoba flavor meshes brilliantly!

Lotte's Koala March (Seasonal): Custard Pudding Flavor

We're bringing you one of Japan's most well known snack, the famous Koala March by Lotte! But this time, it comes in a limited edition Custard Pudding flavor! We hope you enjoy the crunchy biscuit snack that features cute koalas doing a variety of different activities!!

Don't miss out on all the delicious Japanese goodies and subscribe now!
