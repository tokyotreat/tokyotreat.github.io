Celebrating TokyoTreat's 2nd Anniversary with BIG changes!

06 March 2017 by Vanessa

New TokyoTreat

Dear TokyoTreat family,

Next month is our 2nd anniversary!! We're celebrating with a $5 off forever discount! Use code NEWTOKYOTREAT for $5 off your Premium box every month!

First and foremost, we want to sincerely thank you for helping us get to where we are today.

2 years ago, we embarked on a mission to introduce fun and crazy Japanese candies to Japan-lovers all over the world! When we first started off, we were a few people in a small office, with hopes and visions, positive and high. Since then, we've expanded and have come along way. While it is real fun to look back, it is equally exciting to look forward, which matters the most. Now we're ready to roll out several BIG improvements that will make your experience significantly better!

More candy, new Dagashi bags, BIGGER boxes, faster shipping & more!

More snacks, same price!

We're including more candies in your box, because why not!? Our increased box size allows us to include more candies and snacks! Best part is - the price stays the same! Starting April, the structure will be as followed:

We've also strengthened our partnerships with existing manufacturers which allows us to increase quantity without compromising quality. Not only that, we're also partnering with new snack manufacturers that will let us bring you exclusive Japanese flavors and brands to your boxes! Let us know in the comments what snacks and brands you'd like to see next!

All plans now include a Dagashi Bag and Wagashi in Reg & Prem

We know many of our subscribers love the major brand Japanese snacks we include in our boxes. However, in Japan, there still exists many type of snacks that we wanted to share with you all. That said, we decided to include a Dagashi Bag that comes with 5 mystery snacks in ALL plans starting this April. With the dagashis, we’re taking you a trip down the memory lane with the old-fashioned post-world war Japanese candies! You might even be able to find snacks you've seen on the hit anime Dagashi Kashi!

If you're also interested in traditional Japanese sweets, wagashi, you'll be happy to know that now all Regular and Premium plans will come with a Wagashi treat! From azuki bean Yokan to soy-sauce flavored rice cracker, look forward to the wagashi treat with subtle taste that'd go perfectly with your afternoon tea! There are so many types of traditional Japanese snacks we can't wait to share with you all!

Everyday shipping!

Being based in Tokyo, we work closely with Japan Post in the port area (Minato-ku) to ship your boxes reliably and faster. With the increasing number of TokyoTreat group subscribers, Japan Post has doubled the delivery schedule, staffing and trucks to process our boxes. This means faster shipping times for all subscribers.

Not only that, we now apply everyday shipping, you don’t have to wait until the following month to get your boxes shipped. The earlier you subscribe, the earlier you get your boxes! And as always, the SHIPPING IS ALWAYS FREE WORLDWIDE for all plans!

Bigger, sturdier boxes

Not only did we completely redesign our boxes, but it's also bigger and sturdier than ever! The box lid is designed to fully isolate the sides to ensure the security and prevents snacks from falling out. No more damaged candy! The increased box size allows us to fit more snacks, candies and drinks, this way you get the best value possible!

Improved Website and Customer Portal for better Subscription Management

Have you visited our new site yet? We have a new design, layout and content to enhance your user experience! The new site is designed specifically for ease of use, so you can find exactly what you need quickly and conveniently!

We’ve also moved our Customer Portal to a dedicated server in New York and rebuilt the code from scratch. We've benchmarked it and it resulted in 10x faster loading time. You can upgrade/downgrade, skip and reactivate your subscription smoother with the new Customer Portal.

Which brings us to this question: What’s next?

We're always working on new improvements! If you have any suggestions or comments, let us know down below!

Last but not least, a sincere thank you to all our amazing subscribers for your trust and support, as you provide us with constant motivation to reach new heights!

Don't forget to use code NEWTOKYOTREAT at checkout to get $5 off your Premium box as long as you are subscribed

**If you're a current TokyoTreat subscribers, you can contact our customer support at weloveyou@tokyotreat.com to apply the coupon code.

(Valid until March 31st)
