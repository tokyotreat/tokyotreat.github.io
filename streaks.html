Frequently Asked Questions

Streaks are monthly rewards that you receive for staying subscribed. As long as you do not cancel or skip a month, you streaks will continue to grow. As your streaks grow, you will earn awesome rewards!

In order to extend your streak, you need to activate your box. When you receive your box, there will be a unique code on your box’s label that you’ll input on your customer portal. Once your box is activated, your streak will extend. When you reach a certain checkpoint, you can redeem your rewards.

The longer you stay subscribed, the more rewards you’ll receive! How awesome is that?

Your streaks will reset if you cancel your subscription or skip a month.
